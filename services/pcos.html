Understanding PCOS

Comprehensive PCOS Care

Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) is one of the most common hormonal disorders affecting women of reproductive age. It can cause a range of symptoms including irregular periods, weight gain, acne, excess hair growth, and fertility challenges.

At Bio Balance, we take an integrative approach to PCOS management, addressing the root hormonal imbalances while providing support for metabolic health, fertility, and overall well-being.
