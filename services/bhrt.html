What is BHRT?

Bioidentical Hormone Replacement

Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy (BHRT) uses hormones that are chemically identical to those your body produces naturally. Unlike synthetic hormones, bioidentical hormones are derived from plant sources and are designed to match your body's own hormones exactly.

As we age, hormone levels naturally decline, leading to a variety of symptoms that can significantly impact quality of life. BHRT can help restore optimal hormone levels and alleviate these symptoms.
