Dr. Ellefsen was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest where he returned almost 20 years ago to begin practicing medicine and raise his family. While he maintains his board certification in Family Medicine, he has spent a decent part of his career providing rural Emergency care to communities surrounding Spokane, Washington.

Two years ago he was reintroduced to the world of hormone optimization and seeing its benefits both personally and among his patients, he has spent the last two years certifying in BHRT mastery through Worldlink Medical.

He and his family enjoy spending time in the outdoors including hiking, backpacking, water sports, snowboarding, and travelling.
